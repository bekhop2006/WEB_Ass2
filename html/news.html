    Head of Zhambyl Region Police Department Bakytzhan Malybayev Resigns

    Published on September 23, 2025 by Beknur Tanibergen

    According to the Ministry of Internal Affairs, Malybayev submitted his resignation report and was relieved of his post by order of the Minister of Internal Affairs. It is noted that Bakytzhan Malybayev was appointed to the position in September 2024.

    Bakytzhan Malybayev was born on July 23, 1969, in the village of Kyzyltas, Korday District, Zhambyl Region. He began his service in the internal affairs bodies in 1990. Throughout his career, he held various positions in law enforcement and was known for his dedication to public safety.

    The resignation comes as part of a broader restructuring within the Ministry of Internal Affairs. A new head of the Zhambyl Region Police Department is expected to be appointed within the next few weeks. The ministry has assured the public that all ongoing investigations and operations will continue without interruption.

    Higgsfield: AI Model for Video Generation Raises $16M

    Published in September of this year by Tanibergen Beknur

    "Higgsfield is a generative artificial intelligence model that creates videos based on text queries. The startup has raised $16 million in investment from renowned Silicon Valley venture capital firms. It currently competes directly with products from major tech giants like Open AI and Google," said Zhaslan Madiyev in April of this year.

    The investment round was led by prominent venture capital firms including Sequoia Capital and Andreessen Horowitz. The funding will be used to accelerate product development and expand the team of AI researchers and engineers.

    Higgsfield's technology represents a significant breakthrough in video generation, allowing users to create high-quality videos from simple text descriptions. The company plans to launch its public beta version in the coming months, with a full commercial release expected by the end of the year.
